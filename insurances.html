Dental Insurance

A core value at Dental Specialty of Houston is providing high-quality dental care to all patients. That's why we accept most dental PPO, HMO, and Texas Medicaid insurance plans. We are committed to making dental care accessible regardless of the type of insurance our patients have. Our team spends many hours working with insurance companies to credential our doctors and ensure patients can receive the care they need.
