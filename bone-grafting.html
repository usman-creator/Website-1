Bone Grafting

Bone grafting rebuilds jawbone support where volume has been lost after infection, trauma, or missing teeth. This procedure creates a stronger foundation for future implant placement.

We evaluate your site with 3D imaging and select a grafting approach tailored to your timeline, anatomy, and long-term treatment goals.
