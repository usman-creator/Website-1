Common Procedures

Extraction

Tooth extraction is recommended when a tooth is severely damaged, infected, or impacted. Our surgical team uses imaging-guided planning and comfort-focused anesthesia options to keep treatment efficient and reassuring.

You will receive clear pre-op instructions and personalized healing support, including post-surgical care guidance and follow-up planning.

Simple Tooth Extraction

A common procedure to remove a decayed, damaged, or loose tooth that is visible above the gum line. This does not require incisions or stitches and has a shorter recovery period.

Wisdom Tooth Extraction

Surgical removal of impacted or misaligned wisdom teeth. This may involve incisions and sutures after the procedure.
